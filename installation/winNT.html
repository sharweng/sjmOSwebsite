Windows NT 4.0 Installation
Windows NT 4.0

Windows NT 4.0 built on the success of its predecessors by incorporating the Windows 95 user interface, making it more familiar and accessible to users. It retained the robust NT kernel, offering superior stability and security for business and server environments. NT 4.0 introduced support for hardware acceleration and improved networking features, making it a popular choice for enterprises and IT professionals. However, its lack of plug-and-play support and higher hardware requirements limited its appeal to casual users.
